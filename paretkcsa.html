#pareTKCSA

Skills: Graphic Design, Code, Storytelling
URL: #pareTKCSA

“Pare TKCSA” is a campaign against TKCSA (ThyssenKrupp Steel Americas). The company operates at Santa Cruz neighborhood, west zone of Rio de Janeiro. The firm had its construction works suspended by the National Environmental Authority (IBAMA) was twice fined due to damages caused to the community’s health and to the environment of the steel industry’s surroundings and today it’s charged with two criminal cases present by Rio de Janeiro’s Public Prosecutions Office. Since 2010 (when TKCSA started the operations) the community of Santa Cruz fight against the company.

In 2015 I was invited to build a website about that history. My role was understand the history; choose forms to represent it and developer the idea in creative and technical way. I choosed Wordpress and Timeline JS to build the project. To run the project I worked in collaboration with 2 journalists, Iara Moura and Janaina Bras who helped me to tell the history.
