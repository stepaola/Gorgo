Steffania
Paola

I’m a Latin American self-taught developer, graphic designer, digital security and code trainer passionate about the intersection of human rights and technology. I'm truly engaged in using my tech skills to fight for an open and free Web. Also, I believe that Internet should be diverse, decentralized and open, that everyone should be entitled to privacy and freedom of expression, that net neutrality is a right and that everyone should have access to the Internet.
