Steffania
Paola

I’m a Latin American self-taught developer, graphic designer, artist, digital security and code trainer passionate about the intersection of human rights and technology. I'm truly engaged in using my skills to fight for an open and free Web. I also believe in a transfeminist internet, that needs to be a safe space for women and trans people.

During the 7 days meeting we created the Information Security Guide Tem boi na linha: www.temboinalinha.org
