MINING:SHARING OUT FEAR,CAPTURING WEALTH

Skills: Code, Data visualization, Graphic design
URL: Map, Infographic

Communities – whether traditional agricultural, fishing, Indigenous people or ex-slave – and workers suffer constant pressure from mining. The mineral extraction, road construction, ports, pipelines, railways, hydro-electric installations and all the other components that connect the mining chain put at risk the physical, social, economic and cultural reproduction of the surrounding areas. In this work I used my skill with code, graphic design and data visualization to show the serious social and environmental impacts causes by mining in many countries. I created an infographic using illustrations from Iconoclasistas and I built a interactive map using Odyssey JS library. This work was commissioned by International Articulation of People Affected by Vale.
